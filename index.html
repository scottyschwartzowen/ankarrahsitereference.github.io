Text Styles

H1: Main page heading

  • Font-weight: 600 (bold)
  • Font-size: 3.2rem or 40px
  • Font-family: DM Sans

H3: Section heading

  • Font-weight: 500 (normal)
  • Font-size: 2.2rem or 25px
  • Font-family: DM Sans Medium

H4: Main page subheading

  • Font-weight: 400 (normal)
  • Font-size: 1.5rem or 20px
  • Font-family: DM Sans

H4: Main page sub-section heading

  • Font-weight: 700 (bold)
  • Font-size: 1.5rem or 20px
  • Font-family: DM Sans
H5: Sub-headline
  • Font-weight: 400 (normal)
  • Font-size: 1.2rem or 18px
  • Font-family: DM Sans
H6: Quotes
  • Font-weight: 400 (normal)
  • Font-size: 1.9rem or 22px
  • Font-family: Caveat

P: Paragraph Text

  • Font-weight: 400 (normal)
  • Font-size: 100%
  • Font-family: DM Sans

B/L: Button & Link Text

  • Font-weight: 500 (normal)
  • Font-size: 100%
  • Font-family: DM Sans Medium
  • Text-decoration: None
